Solid Carbide End Mills

Premium solid carbide end mills for high-performance machining in demanding applications.

  • Sub-micron grain carbide for superior wear resistance
  • Optimized helix angles for different materials
  • Variable pitch designs to reduce chatter
  • Available in 2, 3, 4, and 6 flute configurations
  • TiAlN and DLC coating options

HSS End Mills

High Speed Steel end mills for general purpose machining and cost-effective solutions.

  • M42 cobalt HSS for enhanced toughness
  • Steam oxide finish for improved chip evacuation
  • Center cutting designs for plunge operations
  • Right and left hand spiral options
  • Standard and long length versions

Technical Features

  • Custom end mills designed to customer specifications
  • Manufactured using advanced CNC grinding technology
  • 100% quality inspection with optical measurement
  • Multiple coating options: TiN, TiAlN, AlCrN, DLC
  • Tight tolerances: h6 on shank, ±0.01mm on diameter
  • Precision balanced for high-speed operations

Machining Operations
