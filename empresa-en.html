Our History

Swisstool Industry and Commerce was founded in 1995 by Mr. Markus Niggli. Having the experience from over 20 years in the production of precision tools, Mr. Niggli realized the potential and the necessity of special tools in the Brazilian market.

Taking advantage from the economical stability and the opening of the Brazilian commerce, that facilitated the import of the machinery necessary for the production of tools with high precision and quality, he began the company in Espírito Santo do Pinhal – a small town 200 km from São Paulo – in a small hangar with 500sqm.

Over the years he established the name of the company in the precision tools industry – where Swisstool became synonymous of quality and on time delivery – and keeps growing. Nowadays Swisstool counts with a structure of more than 8.000sqm, with 3.000sqm of buildings, and serves many sectors from the Brazilian market.
