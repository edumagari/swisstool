Helical Drills

Used in machining ferrous materials such as steel, stainless steel, and others.

  • Optimized helical design for efficient chip evacuation
  • Helix angle designed for maximum performance in ferrous materials
  • Available in various dimensions and lengths
  • Options with internal coolant for high performance applications

Straight Flute Drills

Used in machining non-ferrous materials such as brass, aluminum, cast iron, and others.

  • Special geometry for non-ferrous materials
  • Straight flutes for rapid chip evacuation
  • Reduces burr formation in soft materials
  • Excellent surface finish

Technical Features

  • Special custom design and standard drills
  • Manufactured in Carbide and HSS (High Speed Steel)
  • Options with or without internal coolant
  • Various coatings: TiN, TiAlN, TiCN, DLC
  • Strict dimensional tolerances according to DIN standards
  • Resistance to high temperatures and wear
